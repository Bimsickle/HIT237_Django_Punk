Punk Music

Punk music is the backbone of civilised anarchy...

Punk rock music usually consists of short, fast-paced songs, stripped down instrumentals and are often very political or more notably have anti-establishment themed lyrics. Whilst this site has slightly modern bands as the default data, that is not to discredit to earlier punk bands and their music.

To have a Punk Rock Band, or any band for that matter, you first need Musicians. To then be able to make punk music, the Musicians need some skills, usually vocal or instrumental. For the joys of punk music to be shared by the masses that are not at a gig, the Band must record an album, with Musicians from the band, or guests. Finally, every album is made up of songs for you to enjoy and if you are lucky, there may be a video for you to ponder.
